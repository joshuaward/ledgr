What it does

Smart budgeting, zero friction.

Built around how people actually get paid — every two weeks — not abstract monthly averages.

⚡
Pay-period allocation engine
Every paycheck is automatically allocated forward — covering bills due in the next 16 days, groceries, allowance, sinking funds, and savings goals in priority order.
✦
AI document import
Drop a pay stub or bill statement. Claude reads it and extracts income and bill data automatically — net pay, employer, due dates, amounts. You review and apply.
◎
50 / 30 / 20 lens
Every paycheck is measured against the 50/30/20 benchmark — needs, wants, savings. Colour-coded and tracked over time so you see the trend, not just the snapshot.
◈
Sinking funds
Set money aside each paycheck for predictable-but-irregular expenses. Annual insurance, car registration, holiday gifts. The bill arrives. You feel nothing.
⊕
Multiple budgets
Create separate budgets for different scenarios — household, personal, partner. Switch between them instantly from the sidebar. Each is fully isolated.
◐
Light & dark themes
A considered design system with BEM-structured SCSS, CSS custom properties for runtime theming, and Fraunces serif type — opinionated but not over-designed.
